Personal Loan

  1. Completion time:

    • The pre-screening application process which will determine whether you qualify for an Bassie Financial Personal loan will take about 2 minutes.
    • After pre-screening and you are not declined, it will take about 8 minutes to complete the rest of the loan application.
    • The application process will be as follows:
      1. Complete information required for Quick Scoring,
      2. The system will score your application,
      3. The outcome of the score will be displayed,
      4. If application is not declined, then continue with application,
      5. Process your application,
      6. The outcome is displayed,
      7. End of loan application process.

  2. What you need before starting this application:

    • You must be from the age of 18 to 63 years to apply
    • You must be permanently employed for the past 6 months or self employed for the past 12 months
    • You must be a permanent resident of South Africa
    • South African ID book (green, bar-coded)
    • An active bank account for 6 months with electronic salary deposits
    • Proof of income (latest payslip and last 3 months bank statements)
    • A minimum value of R2000 can be applied for
