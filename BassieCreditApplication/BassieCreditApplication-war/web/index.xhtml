Personal Loans

Bassie Financial Service has a solution for you, whether you wish to consolidate your debt, top up an existing loan or apply for a new loan.
18 - 65 years old (you must have repaid the loan by your 65th birthday) and earn R700 monthly after expenses and repaying the new loan.

What the Personal loan offers me

  • Loans between R1 000 and R30 000 with up to 60 months to pay


What this allows me to do:
  • Take-a-break in January from repaying your instalment
  • Settle your account early or make additional payments without any penalties

What documents do I need?

  • Valid Green bar coded South African ID
  • 1 month's payslip / 4 monthly payslip (if paid fortnightly or weekly)
  • 3 month's bank statement for new customers
  • If Self employed 6 months bank statements
  • Proof of residence
